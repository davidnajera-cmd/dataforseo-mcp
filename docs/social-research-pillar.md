# Social + Research as the Second Product Pillar

## Position

Social intelligence is not an addon. It is the second major pillar next to SEO.

Why:

- it captures demand language earlier
- it reveals objections and comparisons before they appear in search tools
- it helps explain why certain content themes or offers resonate

## Local building blocks already present

- `zernio_*` for connected social accounts and analytics
- `social_intel_*` for social analysis flows
- `apify_*` and `scrapegraph_*` for voice, research, and enrichment
- `social-dashboard-data.ts`

## Product direction

### Social intelligence module

Should answer:

- what themes are winning
- which posts are decaying too fast
- what the audience keeps asking
- which comments should become FAQ, landing copy, or content briefs

### Research module

Should answer:

- which competitors dominate narrative share
- what creators and ads are shaping perception
- what adjacent topics are emerging before they peak in SEO tools

## Backlog integration

Every strong social signal should be eligible to create or enrich backlog tasks:

- repeated objection -> FAQ / PDP / landing page task
- recurring desire -> new content cluster task
- strong content theme -> repurpose into SEO page or supporting article
- negative reputation pattern -> GBP / UX / content correction task
