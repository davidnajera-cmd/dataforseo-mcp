# Dashboard Differentiation Backlog

## Principle

A premium dashboard should help a growth lead decide what to do next, not just expose one vendor's payload.

## Current strengths in repo

- SEO dashboard
- social dashboard
- executive overview snapshot storage
- backlog and agent system

## Where the dashboards still look too vendor-centric

1. SEO metrics are strong, but decision layering can be clearer.
2. AI visibility is present, but not yet tied tightly to demand or backlog.
3. Social and Google Business signals are not yet consistently shown as growth drivers next to SEO.

## Proposed top-level modules

### Executive Overview

- growth scorecard:
  - organic clicks trend
  - SEO conversion trend
  - AI visibility trend
  - social engagement trend
  - GBP review/reputation trend
- top risks
- top opportunities
- next best actions from backlog

### SEO

- demand vs coverage
- top losing pages
- CTR repair queue
- indexation risk
- AI visibility by topic

### Social

- channel health
- top content themes
- audience objections and demand signals
- comments requiring content or reputation response

### Local / Reputation

- GBP reviews trend
- top review topics
- location performance changes

## KPI combinations worth prioritizing

- GSC clicks + GA4 conversions + backlog status
- Clarity rage/dead clicks + landing pages losing organic traffic
- social comments themes + keyword gaps + AI fan-out gaps
- GBP review themes + local landing page opportunities
